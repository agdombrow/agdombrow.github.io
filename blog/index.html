Perhaps one of the greatest twists in the tale of Jaws is not found in its suspenseful storyline but in its real-world legacy. The fictional Great White, known for taking lives, inadvertently became a lifesaver through the swell of support for shark conservation it helped to amass. “Swimming Against the Current” isn't just an examination of this legacy; it's a dedication to the enduring mission of those individuals who fight to keep the ocean's balance intact, inspired by the very work that once cast a shadow upon these misunderstood predators.
